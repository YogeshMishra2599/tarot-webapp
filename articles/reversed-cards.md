---
title: Reversed Cards - When and How to Use Them
date: 2026-02-25
excerpt: Whether to read reversals and what they can add to your interpretations.
---

Reversed cards can mean blocked energy, inner focus, or the shadow side of the upright meaning. Some readers use them; others don't — both are valid.

If you're new to tarot, try reading only uprights until you feel comfortable. When you introduce reversals, do it gradually and notice how they change the story of a spread.
