---
title: How to Cleanse Your Tarot Deck
date: 2026-03-01
excerpt: Simple rituals to clear and reconnect with your deck before and after readings.
---

Cleansing your deck helps you and your cards stay in sync. You can use moonlight, a brief pass through incense smoke, or a few minutes of intention-setting.

There's no single "right" method — choose what feels respectful and sustainable for you. Doing it regularly builds a calm, focused practice.
