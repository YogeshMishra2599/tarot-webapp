---
title: Three-Card Spreads for Daily Guidance
date: 2026-02-28
excerpt: Past, present, and future layouts and how to read them with confidence.
---

The three-card spread is one of the most versatile tools. Past–present–future is classic, but you can also use theme–obstacle–advice or situation–action–outcome.

Keep the same positions each time so you develop a consistent language. Trust the first interpretation that lands; you can always add nuance as you go.
