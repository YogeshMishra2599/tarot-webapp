Each Minor Arcana suit is linked to an element: Wands with fire, Cups with water, Swords with air, and Pentacles with earth.

These correspondences help you quickly sense the energy of a reading — whether it's about passion and action (Wands), emotion and relationships (Cups), thought and conflict (Swords), or material and practical matters (Pentacles).

Use this map when you lay out a spread to see which areas of life are most active.
