title: Understanding the Major Arcana
date: 2026-03-10
excerpt: A guide to the 22 cards that represent life's spiritual journey and key archetypes.
---

The Major Arcana forms the heart of the tarot. These 22 cards tell a story from the Fool's first step to the World's completion.

Each card carries deep symbolism and can appear in readings to highlight major themes, life lessons, or turning points. Learning their keywords and imagery will deepen every reading.

Start with the first seven cards — from the Fool to the Chariot — to see how the journey of the seeker unfolds.
