---
title: Numerology Basics in Tarot
date: 2026-03-05
excerpt: What the numbers one through ten add to each card's meaning across the Minor Arcana.
---

Numbers one through ten repeat in each Minor Arcana suit, and each number carries a theme: beginnings (1), choice (2), creativity (3), stability (4), change (5), harmony (6), reflection (7), movement (8), completion (9), and cycles (10).

When you see multiple cards sharing a number, that theme is emphasised. Combine number meaning with the suit to build a clear picture without memorising every card.
