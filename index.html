How It Works

What happens in a reading

When you book a session, here's how your tarot reading works.

01

You share your question

You tell the reader the area you want guidance on — love, career, a decision, or something more general. There are no wrong questions.

You’ll receive a written summary of your reading; a short voice note can be sent too if you prefer.

02

Cards are drawn in a spread

The reader shuffles the deck and lays cards in a spread (e.g. past, present, future). Each position has a specific meaning in your reading.

Your reading is delivered as a written summary, with an optional short voice note to walk you through the cards.

03

Interpretation together

The reader interprets each card in context of your question. You can ask follow-ups and we discuss what comes up.

You’ll get a written summary plus an optional voice note or live chat on WhatsApp — we agree on the format when you book.
